About Snowhill

Built from real roots and real work.

The Snowhill name comes from Snow Hill Township and Snow Hill Lane, where the founder’s uncle operated Snowhill Nursery. It reflects roots in land, work, and earned experience.

The business exists to offer grounded, practical project review shaped by decades of field construction experience, not theory. The goal is simple: help people think through a job before that job turns into a problem.
